Dave "Growl" Johnson

Vocalist

Dave "Growl" Johnson is the voice that drives the band's powerful sound. Known for his deep, guttural growls and emotional range, he brings a raw intensity to every performance, captivating the audience with each note.

Dave discovered his love for music at a young age and was drawn to the power of vocal expression. Influenced by legends like James Hetfield, Bruce Dickinson, and Ronnie James Dio, he honed his unique vocal style that blends classic heavy metal with a modern edge.

Offstage, Dave is a dedicated songwriter, constantly working on new lyrics and melodies. His passion for vocal techniques and sound experimentation has led him to become the band’s unofficial vocal coach, helping other members expand their vocal abilities. When he's not in the studio, he enjoys reading, exploring new vocal techniques, and giving back by mentoring aspiring singers.

With a voice that can go from bone-shaking lows to soaring highs, Dave "Growl" Johnson is more than just a vocalist—he’s the heartbeat of the band. Whether belting out powerful anthems or delivering soul-stirring ballads, when Growl sings, the crowd is always left in awe.
