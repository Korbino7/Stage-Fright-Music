John "Riff" Smith

Lead Guitarist

John "Riff" Smith is the backbone of the band's electrifying sound. Known for his insane solos, blistering speed, and raw energy on stage, he brings a unique blend of technical skill and emotional depth to every performance.

Born with a guitar in his hands (or so the legend goes), John started shredding at the age of seven. Influenced by rock legends like Eddie Van Halen, Slash, and Randy Rhoads, he developed a signature playing style that fuses classic rock with modern metal. His iconic red-and-black custom guitar is almost as famous as his onstage antics, which often include jumping off amps, playing behind his head, and setting his pick on fire (don’t ask how).

Offstage, John is a perfectionist in the studio, spending countless hours fine-tuning every note. He’s also the band’s unofficial gearhead, always tweaking his pedalboard for the perfect tone. When he’s not playing, he’s collecting vintage guitars, designing custom effects pedals, and mentoring up-and-coming musicians.

With a heart that beats in 4/4 time and fingers that move faster than a jet engine, John "Riff" Smith is more than just a guitarist—he’s a force of nature. Whether he's tearing through a face-melting solo or crafting intricate melodies, one thing’s for sure: when Riff picks up his guitar, the world listens.
