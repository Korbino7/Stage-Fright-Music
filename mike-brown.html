Mike "Thunder" Brown

Drummer

Mike "Thunder" Brown is the driving force behind the band's thunderous sound. With every strike of his sticks, he commands the stage, delivering explosive beats and earth-shaking rhythms that keep the crowd on their feet.

Born with a drumstick in each hand, Mike started banging on pots and pans before he could walk. Inspired by legends like John Bonham, Neil Peart, and Dave Grohl, he developed a powerful and precise drumming style that blends technical mastery with raw intensity. Whether it's a high-speed fill or a pounding groove, Mike makes sure every beat hits hard.

Offstage, he's the band's high-energy prankster, always keeping the mood light with his quick wit and endless jokes. But when it’s time to record, Mike is all business, working tirelessly to perfect every snare hit, cymbal crash, and bass drum thump. He’s also a passionate advocate for up-and-coming drummers, often hosting drum clinics and sharing his expertise with the next generation of rockers.

With relentless energy, unshakable timing, and a presence that can shake the rafters, Mike "Thunder" Brown is the heartbeat of the band. When he takes his seat behind the kit, the storm begins, and there’s no stopping the thunder.
