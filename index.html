About Stage Fright

Stage Fright is a high-energy death metal band known for delivering brutal, electrifying performances that fuse the raw intensity of death metal with modern, aggressive vibes. Formed in 2021, they’ve become a rising force in the death metal scene.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings in a small garage to headlining major festivals, Stage Fright's journey is a testament to their dedication and passion for music. Each member brings a unique style and energy, creating a sound that is both powerful and unforgettable.

Our Music

With influences ranging from classic death metal to contemporary metalcore, Stage Fright's music is a relentless assault on the senses. Their debut album, "Face the Darkness," has been praised for its intricate guitar work, thunderous drumming, and guttural vocals.

Join the Fright

Become part of the Stage Fright family by following us on social media, attending our shows, and rocking out to our music. Together, we can face the darkness and emerge stronger than ever.
